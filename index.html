We are RUWACON

The Preferred Construction Company

Ruwacon is a top-tier South African construction firm. Founded in 1999, the company boasts over 25 years of success in the industry.�Since May 2010, Ruwacon has become one of the few top-tier construction firms in South Africa with a majority black ownership.

The company currently has a 9GB and 9CE CIDB rating, is ISO 9001 certified and proudly holds a Level 1 B-BBEE status.

The Ruwacon Group

OUR VISION

“To be South Africa’s most preferred contractor, focused on client satisfaction and quality projects, completed on time and within budget.”

  • Ruwacon’s Construction Division stands as a hallmark of excellence in the construction industry. With a proven track record of delivering high-quality projects, we specialise in a diverse range of construction services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

  • With a commitment to excellence, our team specializes in the construction and maintenance of essential urban and rural infrastructure projects. From roads to water supply and sanitation systems, Ruwacon’s Infrastructure Division brings a wealth of expertise and experience to every project.

  • The Ruwacon Group’s Plant Hire Division is your trusted partner for comprehensive construction equipment solutions. At Ruwacon Plant, we understand the pivotal role that top-notch machinery plays in the success of your projects. Our Plant Hire services offer a diverse fleet of cutting-edge equipment meticulously maintained to deliver optimal performance.

  • At the Ruwacon Group, our scaffolding division stands as a testament to our commitment to delivering unparalleled construction solutions. With a focus on safety, efficiency, and precision, our scaffolding services are designed to elevate your construction projects to new heights.

The group consists of various divisions, namely: Construction, Infrastructure, Plant Hire, and Scaffolding. This diverse structure grants Ruwacon the advantage of seamlessly operating across various sectors within the construction industry.
